Best Blunts to Light

I obviously haven't smoked every kind of blunt out there, but what I have tried from the corner store is what most people buy anyway. Got these three based on flavor, how long it burns, and how smooth it hits.

I recommend Zig Zag brand blunts the most, because they seem to have more obvious flavor and they hit a lot smoother than a Swisher would. I think they burn a little quicker than a Dutch does, but I personally prefer that anyway.

I also want to acknowledge the use of Woods, but I personally don't smoke that much, but when I roll Zig Zags I can usually roll them pretty fat, sometimes pretty close to how big you can make one Wood.

  • Zig Zags
  • Dutches
  • Swishers
